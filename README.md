# Health Science 1 — Test 3 Cram Tool 💉📚

An **offline, single-file** study app for **Continuous Test 3** of Health Science 1 (Bachelor of Nursing). Covers protein synthesis (DNA/RNA), nutrients, metabolism, and the digestive system.

## 🔗 Open it
**👉 [Click here to open the cram tool](https://jeremyspm.github.io/health-science-test3-cram/)**

Works on your phone or laptop — no install, no login. Your progress saves on your own device.
*(Or grab `index.html`, save it, and double-click — it runs fully offline.)*

## What's inside
- 🎯 **Pass Plan** — exactly what to study, in order, to clear 50%
- 📝 **Case Vault** — the case studies + a master answer bank (every nutrient, all 3 digestion tables, metabolism answers, the ketosis core)
- ❓ **MCQ Trainer** — 176 practice questions, answered & explained, with scoring + retry-wrong
- ✍️ **Short Answer** — written/matching questions with model answers (incl. the Practice Online Test 3)
- 🎓 **Exam Mode** — a timed mock paper, auto-marked and scored against the 50% pass line
- 🧠 **Cram Sheet** + 🃏 **Flashcards**
- 🗺️ **Diagrams** — labelled digestive system, intestinal villus, and DNA
- 🔎 **Search** — look up any term across the whole app + a 244-term glossary
- 🤖 **AI Tutor** — turn any term/concept into a ready-to-send tutoring prompt for ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.

## ⚠️ Heads up
- Answers were generated from the practice quizzes and fact-checked, but this is a **study aid, not official material** — if anything clashes with your lecturer, trust the lecturer.
- Made by students, for students. Good luck! 💪 *Ka taea e koe!*
